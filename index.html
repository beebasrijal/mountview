Who we are?

Mount View English Boarding School was established in 2040 B.S in Ghorahi, Dang. It has become a full-fledged higher secondary school running classes from grade one to twelve with both Science and Management faculties. It is one of the reputed private educational institutions which has been serving for three decades. The school has excellent infrastructure supported by state-of-the-art facilities. It has modern academic buildings with spacious classrooms, laboratories, conference hall, administrative offices, a cafeteria and spacious playing grounds for games and sports such as football, etc.

Our Facilities

Health Care

Students with minor ailments and injuries are treated in the school infirmary by qualified staff nurses. Qualified doctors visit the school infirmary at least once a week. In case of serious illness or injuries, students are admitted to the nearest hospital and parents are informed immediately

Library

We here at Mount View believe in book study. We have a separate mini library in every corner of the classroom. We encourgage our students to read the books except from the syllabus too. We also have a separate library in our school with a large varieties of books with different genres.

School Bus Service

The school provides bus transportation sevice to the students within the distance of few kilometers form the school. Guardians should take their children to theirs nearest bus stop or the students themselves should arrive at their bus stop at proper time.

Laboratories

Practical knowledge has been a must in the recent time. We offer our students separate laboratory rooms for different subjects. We have a full-fledged computer lab with a proper internet connection. We also have a fully-equiped physics, chemistry and biology laboratories.

Principal's Message

Mount View English Boarding School has always been at the forefront of education in terms of quality education. Our students have gone forth and excelled in various fields nationally and internationally. This has been made possible with the blessings of our elders, visionary members of the school managing committee, supportive parents and dedicated staff who have relentlessly strived to deliver quality education. Since its inception, we have always been committed to providing high quality education that emphasizes not only gaining knowledge but also focuses on the students’ holistic development. For its continuous improvement, we listen and respond to parents’ concerns and needs of a changing society. Our child-centred approach to teaching and learning at the primary level has become successful in terms of the overall development of the children. We reiterate our commitment to provide a nurturing environment and every facility possible to ensure a high quality education every student deserves in today’s globalized world. We hope to provide you with some useful information about our school and basic information about your children e.g exam result and fee details. Wholesome participation is encouraged to ensure the well-being and happiness of each and every student in our school.

Teknath Adhikari, Principal
